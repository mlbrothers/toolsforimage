Frequently Asked Questions

What is ToolsForImage?

ToolsForImage is a comprehensive online platform offering a variety of image editing and manipulation tools. Our services include image compression, resizing, format conversion, rotation, watermarking, face blurring, and applying various effects.

How do I compress an image?

To compress an image, visit our Image Compression tool. Upload your image, choose your desired compression level, and download the compressed version. This tool helps reduce file size while maintaining image quality.

Can I resize my images?

Yes, you can easily resize your images using our Image Resize tool. Simply upload your image, specify the new dimensions or scale, and download the resized image.

What image formats do you support for conversion?

Our Image Format Conversion tool supports a wide range of formats including JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, and more. You can convert between these formats easily.

How can I rotate my image?

Use our Image Rotation tool to rotate your images. You can rotate images by 90, 180, or 270 degrees, or flip them horizontally or vertically.

Is it possible to add a watermark to my images?

Yes, you can add text or image watermarks to your photos using our Image Watermarking tool. This helps protect your images and add branding.

What kind of effects can I apply to my images?

Our Image Effects tool offers a variety of filters and adjustments including grayscale, sepia, blur, sharpen, and more. You can enhance your images creatively with these effects.

How do I crop my images?

To crop your images, use our Image Cropping tool. You can select the area you want to keep and remove the rest, perfect for focusing on specific parts of an image.

Can I blur faces in my images for privacy?

Yes, our Face Blurring tool allows you to automatically detect and blur faces in your images, helping maintain privacy in your photos.

Are the tools free to use?

Most of our basic tools are free to use. For information about our premium features and pricing, please visit our Pricing page.

How can I contact support?

If you need assistance or have any questions, please visit our Contact Us page. We're here to help!

Is my data safe when I use ToolsForImage?

We take data privacy seriously. For detailed information about how we handle your data, please read our Legal page.

Do you offer bulk processing?

Yes, many of our tools support bulk processing. You can upload multiple images at once for batch editing. Check individual tool pages for more details.

Can I use these tools on mobile devices?

Yes, our website is fully responsive and our tools work on mobile devices. You can edit your images on-the-go!

Do you have a blog with image editing tips?

Yes, we regularly post helpful articles and tips on our Blog. Check it out for image editing tutorials and best practices.

Who is behind ToolsForImage?

To learn more about our team and the story behind ToolsForImage, visit our Our Story and Team pages.

Is ToolsForImage available in multiple languages?

We're working on supporting multiple languages. For current language options, please check our Languages page.

How can I stay updated about new features?

Follow us on social media and check our Blog regularly for updates about new features and tools.

Do you offer API access to your tools?

For information about API access and integration options, please visit our Features page or contact our support team.

What's the best way to resize images for social media?

Use our Image Resize tool to optimize your images for various social media platforms. We offer preset sizes for popular platforms to make the process easier.

Can I convert a PNG to JPG without losing quality?

Yes, our Image Format Conversion tool is designed to maintain image quality during conversion. You can easily convert PNG to JPG and vice versa.

How do I add a logo watermark to my photos?

Our Image Watermarking tool allows you to upload your logo and apply it as a watermark to your images. You can adjust the position, size, and opacity of the watermark.

What's the difference between sharpening and increasing contrast?

Both options are available in our Image Effects tool. Sharpening enhances edge details, while increasing contrast amplifies the difference between light and dark areas of the image.

How can I crop images to a specific aspect ratio?

Our Image Cropping tool offers preset aspect ratios (like 1:1 for Instagram, 16:9 for YouTube thumbnails) as well as custom cropping options.

Can I create a photo collage using your tools?

While we don't have a dedicated collage tool yet, you can use our Image Resize and Image Crop tools in combination to prepare images for a collage.

How do I remove the background from an image?

Background removal is a feature we're currently developing. Keep an eye on our Features page for updates on new tools.
