What is TIFF to JPG Conversion?

TIFF to JPG conversion is the process of changing images from TIFF (Tagged Image File Format) to JPG (Joint Photographic Experts Group) format. TIFF files are often large and retain high image quality, making them suitable for professional photography. In contrast, JPG files are smaller and easier to share, making them ideal for everyday use. Converting TIFF images to JPG allows you to reduce file size while maintaining acceptable quality for various applications.

Why Convert TIFF to JPG?

Converting TIFF to JPG can be beneficial for several reasons:

  • Smaller File Size: JPG files take up less space, making them easier to store and share.
  • Wide Compatibility: JPG is one of the most commonly used image formats, compatible with almost all devices and platforms.
  • Good Quality: JPG format provides a good balance of quality and file size, making it suitable for online sharing and viewing.
  • Faster Loading: JPG images load quickly on websites, improving user experience.
