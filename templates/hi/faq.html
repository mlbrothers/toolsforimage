Frequently Asked Questions

What is ToolsForImage?

ToolsForImage is a comprehensive free online platform offering a variety of image editing and manipulation tools. Our services include image compression, resizing, format conversion, rotation, watermarking, face blurring, and applying various effects completely free and online.

How do I compress an image?

To compress an image, visit our free online Image Compression tool. Upload your image, choose your desired compression level, and download the compressed version. This tool helps reduce file size while maintaining image quality.

Can I resize my images?

Yes, you can easily resize your images using our free online Image Resize tool. Simply upload your image, specify the new dimensions or scale, and download the resized image.

What image formats do you support for conversion?

Our free online Image Format Conversion tool supports a wide range of formats including JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, and more. You can convert between these formats easily for free.

How can I rotate my image?

Use our free online Image Rotation tool to rotate your images. You can rotate images by 90, 180, or 270 degrees, or flip them horizontally or vertically.

Is it possible to add a watermark to my images?

Yes, you can add text or image watermarks to your photos using our free online Image Watermarking tool. This helps protect your images and add branding.

What kind of effects can I apply to my images?

Our free online Image Effects tool offers a variety of filters and adjustments including grayscale, sepia, blur, sharpen, and more. You can enhance your images creatively with these effects.

How do I crop my images?

To crop your images, use our free online Image Cropping tool. You can select the area you want to keep and remove the rest, perfect for focusing on specific parts of an image.

Can I blur faces in my images for privacy?

Yes, our free online Face Blurring tool allows you to automatically detect and blur faces in your images, helping maintain privacy in your photos.

Both options are available in our Image Effects tool. Sharpening enhances edge details, while increasing contrast amplifies the difference between light and dark areas of the image.
