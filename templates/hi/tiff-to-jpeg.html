What is TIFF to JPEG Conversion?

TIFF to JPEG conversion is the process of changing images from TIFF (Tagged Image File Format) to JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group) format. TIFF is known for its high quality and is often used in professional photography, while JPEG is a popular format for everyday use due to its smaller file size and good quality. Converting TIFF images to JPEG can help you save space and share your images easily.

Why Convert TIFF to JPEG?

There are several reasons to convert TIFF images to JPEG:

  • Smaller File Size: JPEG images are compressed, making them easier to store and share without taking up too much space.
  • Wide Compatibility: JPEG is one of the most widely supported image formats, compatible with nearly all devices and software.
  • Good Quality: JPEG format maintains good quality for photographs, making it ideal for web use and sharing.
  • Fast Loading: JPEG files load quickly on websites, enhancing user experience for online viewing.
