What is JPEG to TIFF Conversion?

JPEG to TIFF conversion is the process of changing image files from JPEG (Joint Photographic Experts Group) format to TIFF (Tagged Image File Format). This conversion is important for users who need high-quality images for printing or archiving. Our tool makes it easy to convert JPEG images to TIFF with just a few clicks.

Why Convert JPEG to TIFF?

There are several reasons to convert JPEG images to TIFF:

  • High Quality: TIFF files retain more image detail and are ideal for professional printing and editing.
  • Support for Layers: TIFF format can store multiple layers and channels, making it useful for graphic design and photography work.
  • No Loss of Quality: Unlike JPEG, which uses lossy compression, TIFF files preserve the original image quality.
