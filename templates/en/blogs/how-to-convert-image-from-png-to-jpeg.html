Introduction

When working with image files, choosing the right format is important. Two commonly used formats are PNG and JPEG. PNG files are known for their high-quality and lossless compression, making them ideal for images with transparency, sharp edges, or text. On the other hand, JPEG files are preferred for photographs and images where reducing file size is crucial, even if it means some loss of quality. Converting a PNG to JPEG can help optimize storage without compromising too much on the visual aspect.
