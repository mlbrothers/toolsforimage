Introduction

Image rotation is the process of turning an image around a specific point, usually its center. This manipulation changes the orientation of the image without altering its content. Rotating images can be done in various degrees, such as 90, 180, or 270 degrees, depending on the desired outcome. This technique is essential in photography, graphic design, and social media, where the right orientation can enhance the visual appeal and message of an image.

Rotating an image is crucial for several reasons. First, it allows you to correct the orientation of photos taken at awkward angles, ensuring that your images appear professional. Second, it can help in creating more dynamic compositions, making your visuals more engaging. Additionally, rotating images can aid in highlighting specific elements or improving the overall aesthetic of a design. Whether for personal use or professional projects, rotating images can significantly impact how they are perceived.
