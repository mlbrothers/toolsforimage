What is ICO to TIFF Conversion?

ICO to TIFF conversion is the process of changing image files from ICO format to TIFF format. TIFF (Tagged Image File Format) is a flexible, adaptable file format for handling images and data within a single file. This conversion is useful when you need to use icon images in contexts where TIFF is preferred or required.

Why Convert ICO to TIFF?

There are several reasons to convert ICO images to TIFF:

  • High Quality: TIFF is known for its high image quality and is often used in publishing and printing.
  • Lossless Compression: TIFF supports lossless compression, preserving image quality.
  • Flexibility: TIFF can store both bitmap and vector images, making it versatile for various uses.
