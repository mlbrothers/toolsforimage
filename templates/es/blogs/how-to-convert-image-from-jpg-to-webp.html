Introduction

When it comes to image formats, two common options are JPG and WebP. Many users often wonder about the differences between these formats, especially the JPG and WebP. JPG, short for Joint Photographic Experts Group, is a widely-used format known for its ability to compress images without significantly losing quality. On the other hand, WebP is a modern image format developed by Google, designed to deliver high-quality images at smaller file sizes, making it an ideal choice for web use.

Given these advantages, many people seek to convert JPG to WebP. To convert JPG to WebP, you can use various tools available online that offer a JPG to WebP converter.
